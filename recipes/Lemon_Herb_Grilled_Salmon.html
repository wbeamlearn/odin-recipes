Lemon Herb Grilled Salmon

Description

A light and zesty grilled salmon dish infused with fresh herbs and citrus, ideal for a quick weeknight dinner or a summer gathering

Prep Time and Servings

Preparation Time:

15 minutes

Cook Time:

10 minutes

Total Time:

25 minutes

Yield:

4 servings

Nutrition Facts

(per serving)

320

Calories

18g

Fat

4g

Carbs

36g

Protein

Ingredients

(for 4 servings)

  • Fish: 4 salmon fillets
  • Vegetables & Herbs: 1 lemon (sliced), 2 sprigs fresh dill, 2 sprigs fresh parsley, 2 cloves garlic (minced)
  • Pantry & Seasoning: 2 tablespoons olive oil, ½ teaspoon salt, ¼ teaspoon black pepper, 1 teaspoon honey

Steps

  1. Prepare the Salmon: Pat the salmon fillets dry with paper towels to ensure a good sear.
  2. Make the Marinade: In a small bowl, whisk together the olive oil, minced garlic, honey, salt, and black pepper.
  3. Marinate: Brush the mixture generously over both sides of the salmon fillets. Let them sit for 10 minutes at room temperature.
  4. Prep the Grill: Preheat your grill or grill pan to medium-high heat. Lightly oil the grates to prevent sticking.
  5. Grilling: Place the salmon skin-side down (if skin is on) on the grill. Cook for 6-8 minutes without moving it to get crispy skin. Flip carefully and cook for another 2-4 minutes until the fish flakes easily with a fork.
  6. Garnish: Remove from heat, top with fresh dill, parsley, and lemon slices before serving.
