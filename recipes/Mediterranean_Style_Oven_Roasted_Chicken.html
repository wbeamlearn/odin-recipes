Mediterranean-Style Oven Roasted Chicken

Description

This recipe delivers a rustic, succulent, aromatic oven-roasted chicken dish featuring North African-inspired spices, perfect for a family dinner.

Prep Time and Servings

Preparation Time:

25 minutes

Cook Time:

1 hour 30 minutes

Total Time:

1 hour 55 minutes

Yield:

5 servings

Protein

Ingredients

(for 5 servings)

  • Chicken: 3 chicken thighs and 2 chicken breasts (skin removed)
  • Vegetables: 3 potatoes, 2 tomatoes, ½ onion, 1 bell pepper, 1 chili pepper
  • Pantry & Seasoning: 2 handfuls of sliced olives, 1 handful of fresh parsley, ½ tablespoon salt, 1 tablespoon cooking oil
  • Spice Mix: 1 teaspoon total of a blend containing Cumin, Ras el-hanout, black pepper, and red pepper

Steps

  1. Prepare the Chicken: Ensure the skin is completely removed from the drumsticks and breasts. Pat them dry.
  2. Chop the Veggies: Slice the potatoes into rounds or wedges. Dice the tomatoes and thinly slice the onion and bell pepper. Finely chop the parsley.
  3. The Marinade: In a large mixing bowl, combine the cooking oil, salt, and your spice mix. Add the chicken pieces and toss until evenly coated.
  4. Assemble the Tray:
      - Layer the potatoes and onions at the bottom.
      - Place the seasoned chicken on top.
      - Scatter the tomatoes, bell peppers, chili pepper, and sliced olives over the gaps.
      - Sprinkle half of the fresh parsley over the dish.
  5. Roasting: Cover the dish with foil for the first 45 minutes to trap the steam (this keeps the skinless chicken moist). Remove the foil for the remaining 45 minutes to let the juices reduce and the potatoes soften.
  6. Garnish: Once the chicken is tender and the potatoes are golden, remove from the oven and top with the remaining fresh parsley.
